About

The story behind ChainSheet

ChainSheet was built out of a simple frustration: supply chain management software is either too expensive, too complex, or not designed for growing businesses worldwide.

🌍

Built for teams worldwide

Most supply chain tools are built for large enterprises with big budgets. ChainSheet is designed to work for growing businesses everywhere — from solo consultants to mid-size operations teams across any industry or country.

📊

Browser-first philosophy

No downloads, no installs. ChainSheet runs entirely in your browser with live formulas — inventory, procurement, forecasting, KPIs, logistics and more. Your data saves automatically to your account.

🔒

Your data, protected

All data entered in the workspace is saved securely to your account and encrypted at rest. Your data is yours — export or access it anytime.

🚀

Currently launching

ChainSheet is in its early launch phase. We are actively improving the platform based on feedback from our first users. Have a suggestion? Email us at support@chainsheet.co
