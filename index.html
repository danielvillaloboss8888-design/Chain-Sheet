About

The story behind ChainSheet

ChainSheet was built out of a simple frustration: supply chain management software is either too expensive, too complex, or not designed for teams in Central America and Latin America.

🌍

Built for teams worldwide

Most supply chain tools are built for large enterprises with big budgets. ChainSheet is designed to work for growing businesses everywhere — from solo consultants to mid-size operations teams across any industry or country.

📊

Excel-first philosophy

Your team already knows Excel. ChainSheet gives you professional-grade supply chain templates that work in Excel, Google Sheets, or directly in our browser workspace — no new software to learn.

🔒

Your data, protected

All data entered in the web workspace is saved securely to your account and encrypted at rest. The formula logic stays on our platform so your workflows are always protected.

🚀

Currently launching

ChainSheet is in its early launch phase. We are actively improving the platform based on feedback from our first users. Have a suggestion? Email us at support@chainsheet.co
